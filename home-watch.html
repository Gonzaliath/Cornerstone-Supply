Home Watch

Keep your property secure and maintained while you’re away. Our Home Watch service ensures your home, cabin, or rental stays in top condition.

Tier 1: Basic Watch

  • Security walk of perimeter (check doors and windows)
  • Check exterior for storm damage
  • Clear mail and packages from porch & mailbox
  • Check for pest or varmint intrusion
